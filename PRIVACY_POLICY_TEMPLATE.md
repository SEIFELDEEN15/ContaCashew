# Privacy Policy for CashTrack

**Last updated: [INSERT DATE]**

## Introduction

CashTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you use CashTrack, we may collect:

- **Email Address**: When you sign in with Google Sign-In
- **User ID**: A unique identifier associated with your account
- **Profile Information**: Name and profile picture from your Google account (if using Google Sign-In)

### 2. Financial Information
- **Transaction Data**: Income, expenses, transfers, budgets, and categories you create
- **Account Information**: Bank account names, balances, and related financial data you manually input
- **Investment Data**: Investment information you choose to track
- **Budget Data**: Budget categories and amounts you set

### 3. Device Information
- **Device Type**: Model and operating system version
- **App Usage Data**: Features you use and how you interact with the app
- **Photos**: Images you attach to transactions (stored locally or in your cloud storage)

### 4. Automatically Collected Information
- **File Timestamps**: For app functionality
- **System Boot Time**: For scheduling notifications
- **Storage Space**: To ensure proper app functionality
- **Local Preferences**: App settings and preferences stored locally

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Store and sync your financial data across devices
   - Display charts, statistics, and financial insights
   - Enable budget tracking and notifications

2. **Authentication**
   - Verify your identity through Google Sign-In
   - Secure your account and data

3. **App Improvements**
   - Understand how users interact with features
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

4. **Notifications**
   - Send budget alerts and reminders
   - Provide transaction notifications (if enabled)

## Data Storage and Security

### Cloud Storage (Firebase)
- Your financial data is stored in **Google Firebase Firestore**
- Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
- Data is encrypted at rest on Firebase servers
- Access is protected by Firebase Authentication

### Local Storage
- Transaction photos are stored locally on your device
- App preferences and settings are stored locally
- You can export your data at any time

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Biometric authentication support (Face ID/Touch ID)
- Secure sign-in through Google OAuth 2.0
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We MAY Share Data:

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**: View all data stored in the app
2. **Export Your Data**: Download your financial data in CSV format
3. **Delete Your Data**:
   - Delete individual transactions or categories
   - Delete your entire account and all associated data
4. **Opt-Out of Notifications**: Disable notifications in app settings
5. **Revoke Access**: Disconnect Google Sign-In and delete your account

### How to Delete Your Account
1. Open CashTrack app
2. Go to Settings → Account
3. Select "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
